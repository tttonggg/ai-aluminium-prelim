# Microstructure Prediction & Analysis

## Tasks
- Grain segmentation (EBSD, optical micrograph)
- Dendrite segmentation (deep etching, X-ray CT)
- Phase identification (XRD, SEM-EDS)
- Porosity characterization (X-ray CT)
- Recrystallization fraction (EBSD)

## Datasets
- **DREAM.3D** — synthetic microstructure generation
- **MicroLib** — small library of metal micrographs
- **No large public Al-microstructure dataset** (most groups have private)
