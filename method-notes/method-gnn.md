# Graph Neural Networks for Alloy Design

## Core idea
Represent atomic structures as graphs; train NN to predict properties from
graph structure. Key for inverse design: target property → composition.

## Landmark references
- Xie & Grossman (2018), Crystal Graph CNN, Phys. Rev. Lett.
- Park & Wolverton (2020), DOI: `10.1103/PhysRevMaterials.4.063801`
- Choudhary et al. (2022), DOI: `10.1038/s41524-022-00734-6`

- Taheri-Mousavi et al. (2024), arXiv `2406.17457`
