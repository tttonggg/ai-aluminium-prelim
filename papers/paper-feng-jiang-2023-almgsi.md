# Simultaneous enhancement in mechanical and corrosion properties of Al-Mg-Si alloys using machine learning

**Authors:** Xinming Feng, Zhile Wang, Lei Jiang, Fan Zhao, Zhihao Zhang (USTB)
**Venue:** *Journal of Materials Science and Technology*
**Year:** 2023
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.jmst.2023.04.072](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jmst.2023.04.072)
**Cited by:** 88

## One-line takeaway
ML-driven simultaneous optimization of mechanical *and* corrosion properties
in 6xxx-series Al-Mg-Si alloys (the workhorse alloy for extrusion, automotive,
and architectural applications). 88 citations = a foundational paper for the
6xxx family.

## Why it matters
- 88 citations in 3 years
- 6xxx (Al-Mg-Si) is the most industrially important Al alloy family
- Multi-property (mechanical + corrosion) optimization
- Direct industrial relevance

## Method
- Machine learning model
- Multi-property optimization: mechanical + corrosion
- 6xxx Al-Mg-Si family

## Open opportunity
- Extend to other 6xxx variants (6061, 6063, 6082)
- Couple with extrusion process optimization (the actual use case)
