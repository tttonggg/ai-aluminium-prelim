# Hot Tearing

## Phenomenon
Cracks form during last stages of solidification when liquid film between
grains can't accommodate thermal contraction. Major defect in DC casting and
some castings.

## Predictors
- Composition (Cu, Mg, Si, Fe content)
- Cooling rate
- Grain refinement
- Restraint geometry
