# Hot Extrusion of Aluminium

## Process
Heated aluminium billet (6xxx, 7000-series) pushed through a steel die at
high pressure, emerging as a continuous profile of fixed cross-section.
Temperature: 400-500°C, pressure: 50-150 MPa, speed: 5-50 mm/s.

## Key process parameters
- Billet temperature
- Die temperature
- Ram speed / extrusion ratio
- Die design (bearing length, land geometry, pocket)
- Container temperature
- Quench rate at exit

## Key quality metrics
- **Surface quality** — picks, die-lines, tearing
- **Profile geometry** — twist, bow, dimensional accuracy
- **Microstructure** — grain size, recrystallization, texture
- **Mechanical properties** — yield, UTS, elongation
- **Energy consumption** — kWh per kg
