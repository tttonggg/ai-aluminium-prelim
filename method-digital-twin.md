# Digital Twin & Surrogate Models

## Core idea
Replace expensive FEM/CFD simulations with fast ML surrogates that can run in
real-time on the plant floor.

## Landmark references
- Fuller, Fan, Day et al. (2020), IEEE Access
  DOI: `10.1109/access.2020.2998358`
- Rasheed, San, Kvamsdal (2020), IEEE Access
- Barricelli et al. (2019), IEEE Access

## Aluminium-specific (limited)
- Most digital-twin work in metals is steel-focused.
- Al-extrusion digital twins exist as concept papers + vendor marketing
  (Sapa, Hydro), few academic papers.
