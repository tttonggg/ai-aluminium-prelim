# Gap: Closed-loop Digital Twin Deployment

## The gap
Aluminium extrusion digital twins exist as:
- Concept papers (academia)
- Vendor marketing (Siemens, Honeywell, Andritz)
- Pilot projects (likely, but unpublished)

What's missing: verified academic paper showing a *closed-loop* digital twin
that takes sensor data → updates model → adjusts process parameters in real
time on a real Al extrusion line.

## Why it matters
- "Digital twin" is one of the most over-used buzzwords
- The industry is buying software on promises
- A real deployment paper would shift the field

## Open opportunity
- Industrial-academic partnership
- Pre-registered protocol
- Open publication of architecture, code (or at least interface specs)
